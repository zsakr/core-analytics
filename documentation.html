Getting Started

Welcome to Ignite Analytics! This guide will help you get started with our match analysis platform.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Minimum screen resolution: 1280x720

Account Setup

  1. Sign up for an account using your email
  2. Verify your email address
  3. Complete your profile setup
  4. Choose your subscription plan

Uploading Matches

Learn how to upload and manage your match videos for analysis.

Supported Formats

  • Video: MP4, MOV, AVI
  • Maximum file size: 2GB
  • Recommended resolution: 1080p

Upload Process

  1. Navigate to the Upload section
  2. Select or drag-and-drop your video file
  3. Fill in match details
  4. Click "Upload and Analyze"
