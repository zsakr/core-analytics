Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Core Analytics and how it can help improve your squash game.

Getting Started

What is Core Analytics?

Core Analytics is an AI-powered platform designed specifically for squash players and coaches. It analyzes match footage to provide detailed insights about player performance, technique, and strategy.

How do I start using Core Analytics?

Simply sign up for an account, choose your subscription plan, and start uploading your match footage. Our AI will analyze your games and provide comprehensive insights within minutes.

What equipment do I need?

You only need a smartphone or camera to record your matches, and a device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) to access the Core Analytics platform.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a free trial period so you can experience the full power of Core Analytics before committing to a subscription.

Do I need special camera equipment?

No, our AI works with standard smartphone cameras. However, for best results, we recommend recording from an elevated position with a clear view of the entire court.

Analysis & Features

What kind of analysis does Core Analytics provide?

We provide detailed analysis of shot selection, court movement, tactical patterns, and technical execution. You'll receive personalized insights and recommendations for improvement.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of professional matches and achieves over 95% accuracy in shot detection and player movement analysis.

Can I track my progress over time?

Yes! Core Analytics provides detailed progress tracking, showing improvements in various aspects of your game through easy-to-understand visualizations and metrics.

How long does it take to analyze a match?

Most matches are analyzed within 15-20 minutes, depending on the length of the footage. You'll receive a notification when your analysis is ready.

Can I share my analysis with others?

Yes, you can share your analysis with coaches, teammates, or other players. Our platform includes collaboration features for easy sharing and feedback.

Teams & Coaching

Can coaches use Core Analytics for their students?

Yes, we offer special team plans for coaches and academies, allowing them to manage multiple players and provide personalized feedback based on AI insights.

How can Core Analytics benefit my academy?

Core Analytics helps streamline coaching by providing objective data, tracking player development, and identifying areas for improvement across your entire team.

Is there a limit to the number of players I can manage?

Our team plans are flexible and can accommodate academies of any size. Contact us for custom solutions for larger organizations.

Can I integrate Core Analytics with other coaching tools?

Yes, we offer API integration options for enterprise plans, allowing you to connect Core Analytics with your existing coaching and management tools.

Privacy & Security

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All match footage and analysis are stored securely and are only accessible to authorized users.

Who owns the match footage I upload?

You retain full ownership of all content you upload to Core Analytics. We never share or use your footage without your explicit permission.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, you have full control over your data. You can delete individual matches or your entire account at any time.

Pricing & Plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there any long-term commitments?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitments required. Enterprise plans may have custom terms.
